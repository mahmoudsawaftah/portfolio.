

# Personal Portfolio Website for Mahmoud Sawaftah

A sleek, dark-mode single-page portfolio with glassmorphism effects and smooth animations.

## Design & Style
- **Dark theme** with deep navy/slate background and subtle gradients
- **Glassmorphism** cards with backdrop blur and translucent borders
- **Smooth scroll** navigation and fade-in animations on scroll
- **Mobile-first** responsive design
- **Color accent**: A modern blue/cyan gradient for highlights and CTAs
